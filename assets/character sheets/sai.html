Your moves
Bo staff & unarmed strikes. You fight with quick blows. The staff hits for 1d8 + 3, a fist or kick for 1d4 + 3, both at +5 to hit.
Flurry of Blows. Right after you Attack, spend 1 ki to add two more strikes as a bonus action (each +5, 1d4 + 3). Your signature burst.
Open Hand Technique. On a hit from your Flurry you may knock the target prone, shove it 15 feet away, or stop it taking reactions. Choose per hit.
Patient Defense. Spend 1 ki to Dodge as a bonus action. Every attack against you has disadvantage until your next turn.
Step of the Wind. Spend 1 ki to Dash or Disengage as a bonus action and leap twice as far. You are always the fastest in the room.
Deflect Missiles. As a reaction you catch or swat aside an incoming arrow or thrown weapon, greatly reducing its damage.
Elemental Strike. Spend 1 ki to sheathe a strike in thunder for an extra 1d6 thunder damage. A booming finisher when you need extra punch.
Features & traits
Ki. You have 3 ki points that fuel Flurry of Blows, Patient Defense, Step of the Wind, and Elemental Strike. They refill on a rest.
Loxodon Serenity. You have advantage on saves against being charmed or frightened. Flattery will get you nowhere.
Unarmored Movement. Wearing no armour, you move 10 feet faster.
Keen smell & trunk. A sharp nose, and a handy trunk for holding a torch, grabbing a key, or pouring the tea.
