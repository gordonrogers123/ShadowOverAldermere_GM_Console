Enemy cutouts -- villains and monsters shown in cinematic shots.

Drop transparent PNGs (alpha channel, cropped to the figure) here. They appear
under "Enemies" in the scene builder's left/right character pick lists. Rescan
after changes (GM "Rescan assets" button, or scripts/sync-assets.sh).

(This is for full-figure cutouts in dialogue/cinematic scenes. Round map tokens
are separate -- those live in assets/tokens/ and are defined in data/cast.js.)
