Ambience loops go here (rain, crowd, wind, crackling fire): .mp3 .ogg .wav .m4a .webm

Drop files in, then click "Rescan assets" in the GM window (serve.py running)
or run ./scripts/sync-assets.sh to regenerate data/manifest.js.

Several ambience loops can play at once under one music bed.

Synthesized placeholder loops ship as a demo (wind, tavern), made by
scripts/gen_demo_audio.py. Replace them with your own audio.
