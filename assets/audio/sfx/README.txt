One-shot sound effects go here (door slam, sword clash, thunder): .mp3 .ogg .wav .m4a .webm

Drop files in, then click "Rescan assets" in the GM window (serve.py running)
or run ./scripts/sync-assets.sh to regenerate data/manifest.js.

SFX are fired once on cue from the GM panel; they do not loop.

Synthesized placeholder one-shots ship as a demo (chime, door-thud, sword-clash),
made by scripts/gen_demo_audio.py. Replace them with your own audio.
